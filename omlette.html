INGREDIENTS

  • Eggs (2)
  • cheese (50gm)
  • onion (1)
  • olive oil(10ml)
  • butter(5gm)
  • turmeric powder
  • red chilli powder
  • coriander
  • salt
  • pepper

STEP BY STEP INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Grate cheese.
  2. Chop onion finely, crush garlic coarsely.
  3. In a pan toss paneer with butterand garlic till crisp and set aside, add little salt.
  4. Saute onion in oil with turmeric and red chilli powder, add little salt and set aside.
  5. Beat eggs with a beater till fluffy, add salt and pepper.
  6. Pour this mixture on the Pan.
  7. Put in the onion and grated cheese over the egg, lower the flame and let the omlete set.
  8. Add coriander for garnish and finish the omlete.
