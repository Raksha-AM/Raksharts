🍫🎂Easy Step by Step Chocolaty Molten Lava Cake Recipe🍫🎂

〰〰Why fear when yummy recipe is here😉〰〰

Molten chocolate cake is a popular dessert that combines the elements of a chocolate cake and a soufflé.It is also known as chocolate decadence cake, soufflé, chocolate fondant and even flourless chocolate cake.

preparation time ✅=15 mins
cooking time 🍳= 20 mins
total time ⌛🔄 =35 mins

INGREDIENTS(for 4 servings 🍽)

  • ½ cup maida / plain flour / refined flour / all purpose flour
  • ¼ cup cocoa powder
  • pinch of salt
  • ½ cup powdered sugar
  • ¼ tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • 12 chocolate pieces
  • ¼ cup oil / butter
  • ¾ cup milk
  • 4 small cups (ramekins or aluminium moulds)

STEP BY STEP INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. Firstly, sieve ½ cup maida, ¼ cup cocoa powder, ½ cup powdered sugar, ¼ tsp baking soda, ½ tsp baking powder and pinch of salt.
  2. Add in 1 tsp vanilla extract and ¼ cup oil / butter.
  3. Further add half cup milk and combine with the help of whisk.
  4. Gradually add more milk until batter is well combined and smooth.
  5. Grease the ramekins or aluminium moulds with butter and dust with cocoa powder.
  6. Pour the batter to ¾ of the the prepared moulds and place in 3 chocolate pieces in centre of each cup.
  7. With the help of spoon, gently push the chocolate pieces inside and cover with batter.
  8. Preheat and bake for 15 minutes at 180 degree celsius or 350 degree fahrenheit,allow it to cool for about 5 minutes before removing.
  9. Now scrape the sides with a knife and invert onto plate, pat gently.
  10. Additionally, sprinkle powdered sugar on top to decorate.
  11. Finally, serve eggless choco lava cake immediately with vanilla ice-cream or some berry
